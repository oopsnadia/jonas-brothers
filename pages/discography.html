Discography

STUDIO ALBUMS

IT'S ABOUT TIME

It's About Time is the debut studio album from the Jonas Brothers. It was released on August 8, 2006 by Columbia Records.

JONAS BROTHERS

Jonas Brothers is the self-titled second studio album and the international debut album released by the Jonas Brothers and their first album to be released under Hollywood Records. The album was released on August 7, 2007.

A LITTLE BIT LONGER

A Little Bit Longer is the third studio album by the American band the Jonas Brothers and their second album released on Hollywood Records. It was released on August 12, 2008.

LINES, VINES & TRYING TIMES

Lines, Vines and Trying Times is the fourth studio album by the Jonas Brothers. It was released June 16, 2009, in the US.

HAPPINESS BEGINS

Happiness Begins is the fifth studio album by the Jonas Brothers, released on June 7, 2019, by Republic Records. It was the first album released by the group when they were revived in 2019.
